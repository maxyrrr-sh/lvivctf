What is Capture the Flag?
A capture the flag (CTF) contest is a special kind of cybersecurity competition designed to challenge its participants to solve computer security problems and/or capture and defend computer systems. The game consists of a series of challenges where participants must reverse engineer, break, hack, decrypt, or do whatever it takes to solve the challenge. The challenges are all set up with the intent of being hacked, making it an excellent, legal way to get hands-on experience.
About Lviv CTF
Capture the Flag (CTF) is a cybersecurity competition where teams of participants test their skills byfinding vulnerabilities and exploiting them to retrieve hidden pieces of information called "flags". Eachflag is worth a certain number of points based on the difficulty of the challenge. The team thataccumulates the most points by successfully capturing flags wins the competition.The organizing committee is composed of representatives from the following state and commercial structures:
• National Coordination Center for Cybersecurity National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine
• Ivan Franko National University of Lviv
• State University of Information and Communication Technologies
• Association of Cyber Security Specialists
• Cyber Defense Academy (CDA)
• SoftServe
• Epam
• Intellias.
The CTF competition, held as part of the DES 2024 (Data Engineering and Security) Winter School,attracted enthusiastic participation from students. A total of 82 participants, forming 16 teams, joinedthe competition. These participants represented a diverse range of geographical locations and skilllevels, with students from various Ukrainian higher education institutions as well as students studyingabroad.
Who can participate?
Participation in the competition is free and open to everyone, regardless of the level of knowledge and experience gained. These can be:
 - School students.
 - Students majoring in cybersecurity.
 - Students from any other major who are passionate about cybersecurity.
How to participate?
Form a team from 2 to 5 participants, choose a captain, register, win and receive prizes from the organizers. However, each participant must register individually, then he/she will be added to the team created by the competition organizers. The captains have to come up with a nickname for the team and create a team in Slack by October 31, 23:59.
What will you get?
In addition to the main achievement - knowledge and experience, the winners receive certificates and prizes.
