ABOUT CTFLVIV2025

International Student Cybersecurity Competition organized by Lviv National University and Cyber Defense Army.

What is a CTF?

CTF (Capture-The-Flag) is a team-based cybersecurity competition where participants identify vulnerabilities, exploit them, and retrieve secret data (known as “flags”). Each captured flag earns a certain number of points based on the challenge difficulty. The team or individual with the highest score wins.

Purpose and Skills

The international student CTF competition fosters practical cybersecurity skills, teamwork, and friendly rivalry among students. Participants gain hands-on experience in ethical hacking, programming, and penetration testing techniques.

Challenge Categories
  • Cryptography
  • Forensics
  • OSINT (Open Source Intelligence)
  • Application Security
  • System Exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Internet
  • Legal Policy
Previous Events

Previous CTF editions have shown strong engagement from students. In 2024, the competition was held in a hybrid format with 106 participants and 36 teams registered online. More information can be found at lvivctf.lnu.edu.ua.

The event welcomed high school and university students from cybersecurity-related and other disciplines. Participants tackled 66 challenges themed around defending against hybrid cyber threats.
